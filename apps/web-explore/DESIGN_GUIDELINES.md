# Web Explore: Design Guidelines

This document outlines the conceptual aesthetics and design principles for the 25 distinct, bold routes available in the `/apps/web-explore` experience, now unified by Vysion Analytics’ football-first brand DNA.

## Route 1: Brutalist / Raw (/1)
- **Purpose**: A stripped-down, unpolished interface focusing entirely on crude efficiency and stark contrasts.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Brutalist, utilizing intense typography, visible structural elements.
- **Constraints**: Maintain strict legibility. Performance should be instantaneous.
- **Differentiation**: Massive, overlapping text elements with sharp black/white contrast and unrefined interactions.
- **Fonts**: High-impact sans-serifs (e.g., Impact) paired with monospaced system fonts.
- **Color Palette**: Ultra-high contrast. Monochromatic (Black/White) with Neon Green accent.

## Route 2: Retro-Futuristic (/2)
- **Purpose**: Nostalgic sci-fi appeal for tech-forward users leaning into analytical discovery.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Cyberpunk-adjacent. Wireframes, glowing terminal vibes, interlaced CRT effects.
- **Differentiation**: Visual noise, glowing neon elements, and 'hacking'-style type-on effects.
- **Fonts**: Pixel or Monospace fonts exclusively (e.g., Fira Code).
- **Color Palette**: Dark mode exclusively. Deep space black, vivid cyan, magenta.

## Route 3: Luxury / Refined (/3)
- **Purpose**: Catering to elite, high-tier club scouts looking for premium curation.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Editorial, cinematic, spatial. Fine lines mimicking high-fashion magazines.
- **Differentiation**: Extreme padding/margins, delicate serif pairing, and slow, intentional motion.
- **Fonts**: Elegant high-contrast Serif (e.g., Playfair Display/Bodoni Moda).
- **Color Palette**: Soft, muted neutral tones with Champagne/Gold accents.

## Route 4: Playful / Toy-like (/4)
- **Purpose**: A gamified exploration of scout data, minimizing intimidation.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Neobrutalism or claymorphism. Big shapes, thick outlines, solid drop shadows.
- **Differentiation**: Isometric perspectives, bouncy animations, pastels.
- **Fonts**: Chunky, friendly Space Grotesk.
- **Color Palette**: Bubblegum pink, sky blue, mint green, banana yellow.

## Route 5: Maximalist Chaos (/5)
- **Purpose**: An overwhelming, sensory-rich collage of athletic energy.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Graphic design anarchy. Overlapping layers, Marquees.
- **Differentiation**: Constant background movement, chaotic typographic scaling.
- **Fonts**: Impact, Bebas Neue, Fira Code, Inter.
- **Color Palette**: Acid green, deep violet, hazard orange, stark black.

## Route 6: Tactical Whiteboard (/6)
- **Purpose**: Strategic focus for directors and tacticians.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Blueprints/Chalkboards. Clean lines, schematic overlays, pitch markings.
- **Differentiation**: Draggable tactical markers, dashed pitch lines.
- **Fonts**: Clean Inter and Mono.
- **Color Palette**: Pitch black, striking whiteboard blue, chalk white.

## Route 7: Matchday Scoreboard (/7)
- **Purpose**: Live tension, match-day adrenaline.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Neon stadium scoreboards, floodlights, matrices.
- **Differentiation**: Massive ticking clock, dot-matrix backgrounds.
- **Fonts**: Impact, monospace.
- **Color Palette**: Deep abyss, blinding electric green.

## Route 8: Stadium Concrete (/8)
- **Purpose**: Physical imposing presence of club architecture.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Monolithic brutalism, concrete textures, club red.
- **Differentiation**: Heavy, unmovable blocks, concrete grain overlay.
- **Fonts**: Bodoni Moda, Inter.
- **Color Palette**: Ash concrete, stadium red, deep shadow.

## Route 9: Data Heatmap (/9)
- **Purpose**: Pure data-viz aesthetic for analysts.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Radars, heatmaps, glowing node graphs.
- **Differentiation**: Massive blurred pulse backgrounds mimicking player heatmaps.
- **Fonts**: Inter, Mono.
- **Color Palette**: Jet black, thermal red, grass green, ice blue (heatmap spectrum).

## Route 10: Vintage Program (/10)
- **Purpose**: Traditional scouting legacy, nostalgia.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Paper textures, matchday printed registers, ink presses.
- **Differentiation**: Antique paper noise, double-lined borders, serif focus.
- **Fonts**: Playfair Display.
- **Color Palette**: Sepia, off-white paper, dried blood red ink.

## Route 11: Draft Blueprint (/11)
- **Purpose**: squad building from the ground up.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Cyanotype architectural plans, strict grids.
- **Differentiation**: Literal ruler edge layouts, dashed schematic lines.
- **Fonts**: Inter tight, Mono.
- **Color Palette**: Blueprint cyan, deep navy, chalk white.

## Route 12: Golden Ball Luxury (/12)
- **Purpose**: VIP Executive access.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: High gloss glassmorphism, gold gradients, prestigious.
- **Differentiation**: Glowing shimmers, deep blur, golden ball motifs.
- **Fonts**: Bodoni Moda.
- **Color Palette**: Pure dark, shimmering gold/champagne.

## Route 13: Draft Card (/13)
- **Purpose**: Gamified recruitment focus.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Varsity sports, collectible draft cards.
- **Differentiation**: Interactive 3D card flipping, shield iconography.
- **Fonts**: Impact, Inter.
- **Color Palette**: Varsity blue, cardinal red, white.

## Route 14: Biometric Scan (/14)
- **Purpose**: Medical/Athletic performance analysis.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Cyber-athlete, retinal scans, tracking meshes.
- **Differentiation**: Scanning line animations, fingerprint nodes.
- **Fonts**: Mono.
- **Color Palette**: Pitch black, intense cyan.

## Route 15: Manager's Office (/15)
- **Purpose**: Executive authority.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Wood, leather blotters, brass tacks.
- **Differentiation**: Rich textures, subdued ambient lighting.
- **Fonts**: Playfair Display.
- **Color Palette**: Deep mahogany red, black leather, gold brass.

## Route 16: Training Ground (/16)
- **Purpose**: Raw effort, high intensity metrics.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Neon training bibs, fast motion, intense diagonal slashes.
- **Differentiation**: Aggressive slant layouts, blinding neon accent overlays.
- **Fonts**: Inter Black.
- **Color Palette**: Turf black, neon bib yellow, high-vis pink.

## Route 17: Analyst Room (/17)
- **Purpose**: Total immersion in data streams.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Multi-monitor grid, surveillance feeds.
- **Differentiation**: Borderless grid snaps, moving bar charts, tracking hover states.
- **Fonts**: Mono exclusively.
- **Color Palette**: Monitor grey, terminal green, alert red.

## Route 18: The Tunnel (/18)
- **Purpose**: Pre-match adrenaline and tension.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Deep shadows, concrete cracks, stark lighting.
- **Differentiation**: Intimidation factor, pulsing recording light, deep perspective.
- **Fonts**: Impact.
- **Color Palette**: Shadow black, stark white, emergency red.

## Route 19: VAR / Review Room (/19)
- **Purpose**: Precision verification of talent.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Technical video review UI, recording overlays.
- **Differentiation**: Frame-by-frame UI, grayscale-to-color video interactions.
- **Fonts**: Inter, Mono.
- **Color Palette**: Technical cyan, video black.

## Route 20: Scout Notebook (/20)
- **Purpose**: Organic, boots-on-the-ground scouting.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Torn notebook, grungy ink, stamped approvals.
- **Differentiation**: Handwriting accents, physical paper edges, chaotic notes.
- **Fonts**: Arial Black, Serif, Handwriting blocks.
- **Color Palette**: Paper white, ballpoint blue, yellow highlighter.

## Route 21: Fan Culture (/21)
- **Purpose**: Acknowledging the heartbeat of the club.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Waving terrace flags, matchday stubs, flares.
- **Differentiation**: Massive SVG waving flag background, ticket stub borders.
- **Fonts**: Inter Black.
- **Color Palette**: Forest green terrace colors, ticket white.

## Route 22: Press Room (/22)
- **Purpose**: Media hype, unveiling the next big signing.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Newspaper print styling, camera flashes.
- **Differentiation**: CSS keyframe camera flash overlay, editorial columns.
- **Fonts**: Impact, Serif arrays.
- **Color Palette**: Newsprint grayscale, stark black.

## Route 23: Locker Room (/23)
- **Purpose**: Player-focused identity, team colors.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Selecting kits, team colors bathing the room.
- **Differentiation**: Dynamic interactive background glows based on kit color selection.
- **Fonts**: Inter.
- **Color Palette**: Dark iron, dynamic team primary colors.

## Route 24: VIP Pass (/24)
- **Purpose**: Exclusive scout entry.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Lanyard and physical access badges.
- **Differentiation**: Spring-loaded lanyard drop animation, badge hole punches.
- **Fonts**: Inter Black.
- **Color Palette**: Matte lanyard black, VIP card white, industrial grey.

## Route 25: Organic Pitch (/25)
- **Purpose**: Grounding the metrics in the real game.
- **Aesthetic Direction**: Grass turf imagery mixed with tactical overlays.
- **Differentiation**: Organic grass textures blended with crisp vector circles.
- **Fonts**: Playfair, Sans.
- **Color Palette**: Deep pitch green, chalk white, natural mint.
